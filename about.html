About Innovation Lab Eswatini

Innovation Lab Eswatini is a business innovation and digital strategy consultancy focused on helping businesses improve operations, solve challenges, and prepare for the future through modern thinking and technology-driven solutions.

Our Mission

To help businesses in Eswatini innovate, adapt, and grow through strategic thinking and digital transformation. We provide clarity, direction, and tools to turn obstacles into opportunities.

Our Vision

To become a leading innovation-driven consultancy empowering businesses across Eswatini and beyond — shaping a future where local enterprises thrive in a digital-first economy.

What Makes Us Different

Innovation-Driven

We solve problems creatively using future-focused frameworks, not cookie-cutter templates.

Business-Focused

We prioritize real operational improvement, measurable outcomes, and strategic impact.

Future-Oriented

We prepare businesses for digital growth, emerging tech, and sustainable innovation.

The story behind the Lab

Innovation Lab Eswatini was founded from a genuine passion for technology, innovation, and business transformation. We saw that many local entrepreneurs and small businesses had brilliant ideas but lacked access to modern strategies and innovation frameworks.

“We believe every business — big or small — deserves the insight and tools to compete in a changing world. That’s why we built a consultancy that blends strategic thinking, digital expertise, and a deep commitment to Eswatini’s growth.”
